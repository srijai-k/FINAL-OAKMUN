A Letter from the
Secretary-General

Dear Delegates,

It is with immense pleasure and excitement that I welcome you to the 16th Edition of Oakridge Model United Nations 2026, held on the 24th, 25th and 26th of July.

It is truly amazing that this conference has been a platform for learning, innovation and transformation for 16 years, and continues to inspire with every new edition. Over the years, Oakridge MUN has grown into more than just a conference — it has become a tradition of collaboration, diplomacy, and leadership. This year will be no different; across the three days, you will not only be debating pressing global issues but also building friendships, exchanging ideas, and discovering the power of your own voice.

As Secretary-General, my commitment is to ensure that Oakridge MUN 2026 is a truly memorable experience for every delegate. Together, I, along with the Secretariat, will work tirelessly to create an atmosphere where debate thrives, ideas are respected, and each of you feels empowered to make this conference your own.

My own journey with Model United Nations began at Oakridge — with curiosity, a bit of nervousness, and a strong desire to learn. I hope each of you will use this conference as the starting point of your own journey — one that pushes you to grow, to question, and to realise that each of you is a force to be reckoned with, and no one can tell you otherwise.

The Secretariat and I eagerly look forward to welcoming you to Oakridge International School, Gachibowli, this July!

Shrey Vajpayee

Secretary-General, Oakridge MUN XVI

FAQ

Everything you need to know about Chapter XVI

What is Oakridge MUN and who can participate?

Oakridge MUN (Oakridge Model United Nations) is one of the largest high school MUN conferences in South India. It's open to students from grades 9–12 from schools across the country. Delegates will represent countries or parties and discuss global issues in various UN committees.

How do I register for the conference?

Delegates may register by filling out the form available on our Registration link. After submission, you will receive a confirmation email containing all the essential information and subsequent steps for participation. You will find a link to a payment portal, from which you must acquire a receipt ID to enter in the registration form.

What committees will be available at Oakridge MUN XVI?

Oakridge MUN XVI presents a wide array of both traditional and specialized committees, each tailored to engage delegates of varying interests. The committees featured in this edition will be revealed soon.

What should I bring to the conference?

Bring formal business attire, writing materials, laptop/tablet for research, position papers, and any required documents. Conference materials, meals, and refreshments are provided. A detailed packing list will be shared with registered delegates.

Are there awards and recognitions?

Oakridge MUN XVI recognises exceptional performances in all committees by presenting various awards, which include Best Delegate, Outstanding Delegate, and Honourable Mention. Delegates will be evaluated based on several general criteria, including research and preparation, the quality of their speeches, coherence to policy, negotiation abilities, leadership within the committee, and their contributions to final documentation.

What is the dress code for the conference?

Business formal attire is required for all sessions. Men should wear suits or blazers with dress shirts and ties. Women should wear business suits, blazers with dress pants/skirts, or formal dresses. Cultural attire is welcome during the social evening.

Can I attend if I'm new to MUNs?

Absolutely! Oakridge MUN XVI extends a warm welcome to both novice delegates and experienced participants. For individuals who are new to Model United Nations, we offer a variety of resources such as comprehensive background guides and pre-conference orientation sessions. Throughout the event, our experienced Executive Board members will provide guidance and mentorship to assist you in understanding the procedures and participating with confidence.

What accommodation options are available?

While the conference itself doesn't provide accommodation, we can recommend nearby hotels and hostels for outstation delegates. Many participants arrange homestays or group bookings. A list of recommended accommodations will be shared with registered delegates.

Can I leave the conference and the campus before the dispersal time?

No. In case of emergencies, official documents and permission slips will be required.

How important is it to submit a consent form?

You will not be allowed to participate in the MUN without submitting the consent form, thus it is of utmost importance to submit it.

Will you issue refunds?

We do not issue refunds, unless a medical emergency arises and is supported by reasonable documentation or evidence otherwise.

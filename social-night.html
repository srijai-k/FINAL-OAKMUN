About the Event

Dress up.
Show up.
Make it count.

Social Night at Oakridge MUN is a beloved tradition — a fully themed evening event on Day 2. Delegates shed the formal blazers, embrace the theme, and come together for an evening packed with performances, music, and real celebration.

Every year, delegates say Social Night is the most memorable moment of the conference. It's where friendships are cemented, memories are made, and the real spirit of Oakridge MUN comes alive.

"Every year the theme is different. The energy is always the same. You'll remember this night."

— Past Oakridge MUN Delegate

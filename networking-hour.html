About the Event

Where diplomacy
gets personal.

The Networking Hour at Oakridge MUN XVI is an unstructured, informal gathering designed to give delegates a break from the formality of committee — and a chance to simply connect.

Whether you're looking to find allies for your resolution, swap notes with a delegate from another committee, or just make a new friend — this is your space. Past delegates call it one of the most memorable parts of the conference.

"The best conversations I had at MUN didn't happen in the committee room. They happened in the hour after."

— Past Oakridge MUN Delegate

What to Expect

An hour well spent.

01 — Open Mingling
No agenda. No seating plan.

Walk up to anyone and start a conversation. That's the whole point — and it's easier than it sounds.

02 — Cross-Committee
Broaden your perspective.

Meet delegates from committees you'd never interact with otherwise. The diversity of thought is half the value.

03 — Refreshments
Conversations flow easier.

Food and drinks available. A long day of debate deserves a proper break before the evening.

04 — Icebreakers
Ease in naturally.

Activities and prompts if walking up to strangers isn't your thing — we've got you covered.

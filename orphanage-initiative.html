Our Mission

Beyond the resolution.
Into the community.

The Orphanage Initiative is a cornerstone of what Oakridge MUN stands for. Every year, delegates don't just debate the world's problems — they take a small but meaningful step toward solving them.

Delegates spend time with the children — through games, activities, conversations, and simply being present. It's a two-way experience: as much as the delegates give, they receive something far more valuable in return — perspective.

"You debate resolutions in committee. Here, you become one."

— Past Oakridge MUN Delegate

How to Contribute

Four ways to make a difference.

01 — Donate
Supplies matter.

Stationery, books, games, essentials — a curated donation list will be shared with all registered delegates.

02 — Visit
Show up in person.

Spend time at the children's home. Play games, share meals, tell stories. Your presence matters most.

03 — Fundraise
Committees can contribute.

Committees can organise small fundraising drives before or during the conference. Details shared post-registration.

04 — Spread the Word
Amplify the impact.

Tell your school, friends, and family. More attention means bigger impact for the children's home.

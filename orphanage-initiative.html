Our Mission

Beyond the resolution. Into the community.

The Orphanage Initiative is a cornerstone of what Oakridge MUN stands for. Every year, delegates don't just debate the world's problems — they take a small but meaningful step toward solving them. A visit to a local children's home reminds us that diplomacy, at its heart, is about people.

Delegates spend time with the children — through games, activities, conversations, and simply being present. It's a two-way experience: as much as the delegates give, they receive something far more valuable in return — perspective.

How You Can Contribute

Four ways to make a difference.

01
Donate Supplies

Stationery, books, games, and essentials — a curated donation list will be shared with registered delegates before the conference.

02
Participate in the Visit

Spend time at the children's home. Play games, share meals, tell stories. Your presence matters more than anything.

03
Fundraise as a Committee

Committees can organise small fundraising drives before or during the conference — details shared post-registration.

04
Spread the Word

Tell your school, your friends, your family. The more attention this initiative gets, the bigger the impact we can make together.

Logistics & participation.

Location: A children's home in Hyderabad — exact details shared with confirmed participants.

Who can join: All registered delegates. Participation is voluntary but strongly encouraged.

What to bring: A donation item from the curated list (to be released), comfortable clothes, and an open heart.

More info: Contact the organising team at mun@oakridge.in for questions.
